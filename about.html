About Shannon

Shannon is currently attending Harvard University and lives in Straus dormitory as a freshman. He plans on purusing a joint concentration in Government and Computer Science, hoping to synthesize the two studies as part of his career.

Shannon grew up in Youngstown, Ohio, and is the oldest of five children. His siblings names are Alyssa, Moses, Noah, and Hannah. He attended Urusline High School and graduated as Valedictorian.

Shannon's interests include running, computer programming, theater, policy, and music. Shannon plays the violin, piano, guitar, baritone, and french horn.
